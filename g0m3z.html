Personal

I was born and raised in Oaxaca, Mexico and lived there for 12 years. My mother, sister and I moved to Austin, TX at that time and have been here ever since. I went to High School at Westwood High School. I went to University at Texas State University where I graduated with a degree in Business Management & a Latin American Business Certificate.
