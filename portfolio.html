Austin Animal Center Analysis For this project, we decided to look into the free Data provided by the city of Austin. Specifically, we looked into the Austin Animal Center Data to try and gather insights into pet adoptions. For our first iteration of the project, we focused only on dogs. Do pure breds get adopted faster than mixed breeds? What is the average time a dog spends in the shelter? Which dog has been in the shelter the longest? etc. Take a look at our analysis and find out the results!
Scooters in Austin, Web App For this project, we decided to look into the Scooters Data that exists for the City of Austin. From that Data we built a WebApp using SQL, FastAPI, Uvicorn, Plotly, HTML, CSS, Jinja and deployed through Google Cloud Run. Take a look and check out the findings!

Python API Challenge In this challenge, I used Python to retrieve data from the OpenWeatherMap API to answer the question of, "Whats the weather like as we get closer to the Equator". From the JSON's retrieved, I created several scatter plots to analyze various relationships between Temperature, Latitude, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind Speed.
